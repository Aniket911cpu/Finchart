## TradingView Deep Feature Audit

TradingView has **400+ built-in indicators**, over 100,000 public community indicators, and **110+ smart drawing tools**. Pine Script v6 supports footprint data requests, dynamic for loops, unlimited scopes, and webhook-based automation.

### TradingView Full Feature Inventory

**Charting:**
- 10+ chart types (Candlestick, Heikin Ashi, Renko, Line Break, Kagi, Point & Figure, Range, Column, HLC Area)
- Bar Replay with 9 speeds, autoplay, synchronized multi-chart replay
- Volume Profile, Footprint Charts (Premium/Ultimate)
- Multi-chart layouts (up to 8 simultaneous charts)
- 50+ timeframes from 1 second to Monthly
- Auto chart pattern recognition

**Alerts (Oct 2025 update):**
- Multi-condition alerts
- Webhook delivery, Email + SMS + push
- Crossing, greater/less than, entering/exiting channel

**Data:**
- 10,500+ Bloomberg indices integrated (Oct 2025)
- 50+ exchange connections
- Economic calendar
